Blood Bank Management System

Full Stack Application

The Blood Bank Management System is an application to create an e-Information about the donor who wants to donate blood. Through this application any person who is interested in donating the blood can register himself. More over if any one need blood online can also take the help of this Application. We aim to bridge the gap between blood donors and receivers. We'll be displaying the donor details of those registered with us, to be useful for people who are searching for blood donors.
